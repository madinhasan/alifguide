Devil's Pool✏️

4.7 (2,345 reviews)
Change Image

Voted as one of the ten world's best natural pools by TripAdvisor in 2014; the Devil's Pool, is a phenomenon 'natural pool' at the edge of the Victoria Falls offering a breath taking experience only found in Zambia. It is adjacent to the famous Livingstone Island which is the best vantage point from which to view and experience the Victoria Falls (locally called Mosi-oa-Tunya). The plunge into the pool is exhilarating.✏️

Best time to go✏️

The Devil’s Pool is usually open between mid August and mid January – depending on the water levels✏️
